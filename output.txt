   please Call 6415 (ETSC).

   Ph: 7377 (b) for LTs and Blocks : ETSC (Ph: 6415).
6. Any room related issues, please contact LH management ( LH 102; email: LHC_booking@admin.iitd.ac.in; Ph: 7368).
